Introducing Sandbox Data Generation, this is a new addition to apex tools is based off some work I've been doing at GLA where we are using DevOps and we have a fairly complex data requirements to run applications. One of the key areas I've been trying to ensure is that we are capable of refreshing our Development Sandboxes often to ensure that DevOps doesn't continuously remind me I make errors and add/delete items that are then perpetually visible are removals (lost many hours accidentally including them in deployments only to get errors).
 
I will do a show and tell on this but for content these are the objects / records being generated at GLA post a sandbox being created to ensure we only have a couple of steps to publish a form and start making applications.
 
Outcome Management
Outcome - 14 of them
Indicator Definition - 59 of them
Unit Of Measure - 2 of them
Indicator Assignment - 177 - 1 per Indicator Definition link to appropriate Outcome/Programme and Funding Opportunity
Time Period - 4 of these which are linked to Program to ensure that when an application is created we support testing single period outcome management or multi-year outcome management
 
Programme Grant Management
Programme - 3 of them
Funding Opportunity - 1 funding opportunity per programme linked to ApplicationTimeline and BudgetTemplate
Fund - 1 of them
Programme Fund - 3 of them linked to the appropriate Programme / Funding Opportunities
Budget - 2 budget to support testing single period and multi-year budgets
Budget Category - 28 of them with 14 per budget in line with how their format their budgets
Budget Period - 4 of them a single period for the 1 year and 3 for the 3 year budget template
ApplicationTimeline - 3 of them for use on the Funding Opportunity
 
General Items
Tag Category - 5 key tag categories as we use tags within the application form instead of multi-select picklists
Tag - 61 of these linked to specific tags
Tag Link - 366 of these linking the tags to Programme and Funding Opportunity to support the application process
Check Template Header - 2 of these to support with assessment processes
Check Template - 14 of these linking to the correct template header to ensure we can take an application through to assessment
Help Messages - 9 based on the current setup to ensure we make it look similar to production
Help Message Filters - 3 of them to match production
 
As you can see this is a lot of data, it is all controlled via Custom Metadata Types and CSV files stored within Static Resources.
