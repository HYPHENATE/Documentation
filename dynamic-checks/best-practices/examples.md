# Examples

## Grant Review Readiness

A grants team wants every funding request to go through the same review preparation steps before panel assessment. Dynamic Checks can be configured on the funding request record so staff must work through categories such as eligibility, due diligence, and documentation completeness.

## Fundraising Gift Processing

A fundraising operations team wants high-value gifts to follow a standard sequence of checks. A filtered template header can show only when the donation meets certain criteria, such as value or type.

## Programme Case Handover

A service delivery team needs a consistent handover checklist when cases move between colleagues. Dynamic Checks can show owner-sensitive checklist items on the case record and ensure the handover record is complete before work is passed on.

## Compliance Confirmation

An operations or safeguarding team needs record-based confirmation that required steps were completed. Dynamic Checks gives a visible, structured checklist without forcing teams into a separate workflow tool.
