# Key Benefits

## Core Benefits

- Turns operational checklists into reusable Salesforce configuration instead of hardcoded logic.
- Allows different checklist sets to appear only when the current record meets the right criteria.
- Gives users a guided Lightning experience with progress visibility by category.
- Supports optional flow launch on completion for follow-on automation.

## Why Teams Use Dynamic Checks

- to standardise process completion on important records
- to reduce skipped steps without building separate custom apps
- to make check completion visible on the record itself
- to reuse the same checklist pattern across multiple teams and objects
- to build on [Apex Tools](/apex-tools/README) rather than inventing another trigger and automation framework
