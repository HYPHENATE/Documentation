# Key Benefits

## End-To-End Funding Model

The package includes the core entities needed to manage grant programmes, applicant requests, reviews, awards, budgets, and payments in one Salesforce data model.

## Operational Workflow Support

Packaged flows, LWCs, paths, and invocables support common grant-management processes such as budgeting, payment handling, reviews, and change requests.

## Role And Access Structure

The package includes a substantial permission model for internal and external grant participants.
