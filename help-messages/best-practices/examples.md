# Examples

## Fundraising

On an `Opportunity` record, show a message when the record type is `Donation` and the status is `Pledged` reminding staff to confirm pledge acknowledgment and expected payment timing.

## Grant Management

On an application or grant request record, show a message at a review stage reminding assessors which evidence or scoring notes must be completed before progression.

## Service Delivery

On a case or client support record, display a message when a record reaches a safeguarding or escalation status so staff can follow the correct internal process.
